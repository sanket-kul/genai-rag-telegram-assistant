# Company Policy

## Working Hours
The standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

## Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow ethical practices.

## Remote Work
Remote work is allowed with prior approval from the reporting manager.

## Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves annually, including sick and casual leaves.

## Data Security
All employees must follow company data security policies and avoid sharing confidential information externally.
